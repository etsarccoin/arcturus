Our Services

Fooding

One of the major industry sectors is the fooding industry that is so closely linked with the hospitality industry that it is difficult to isolate them from each other. Travel and tour enthusiast all over the world have to make a lot of transactions in fact, the major chunk of it on fooding. No travel and tour experience is complete without tasting the authentic and local cuisines, special delicacies etc. With Arcturus, it becomes very for easy and seamless for fooding chains, restaurants, hotels etc. to carry out the payments digitally ensuring cashless transactions. The blockchain based cryptocoin can thus pose as a lucrative opportunity for food businesses not just in tourist hotspots but also in local areas to which may also have some authentic or trade-mark dishes that can be a potential source of earning to the hospitality businesses.
